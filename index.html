Soul's Harmony System Guide

Egregor Score

Represents spiritual harmony and positive growth. Ranges from 0-20 with milestones at 5 (Resonant), 10 (Harmonized), 15 (Incarnate), and 20 (Nexus).

Resonance Points

Temporary resource pool (max 10) that resets on long rest. Can be spent to resist despair, empower abilities, or activate bond powers.

Anchored Bonds

Up to 3 spiritual connections. Risk inversion when Despair exceeds Egregor. Positive bonds can become their negative counterparts.

Despair Score

Represents corruption and negative influences. Each threshold attracts different divine entities with unique corruption effects.

The Crawling Choir

Represents madness and forbidden knowledge. Offers power through whispered truths but at the cost of memory and sanity.
